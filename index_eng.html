About Us

The TGME association is made up of young Africans, university students from the African community of Udine. This association pursues the exclusive purpose of social, human, civil and cultural solidarity. The association is apolitical and is non-profit.

Things We Do

The association is responsible for implementing programmes, initiatives and projects for economic development, cultural and social promotion in developing countries and in particular in African countries; to promote awareness and information initiatives in developing countries on the issues of interdependence and relations between the environment and development, on the principles of peace, international solidarity and cooperation between peoples.
