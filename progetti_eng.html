Key initiatives/projects or in the process of being implemented

Dedicated Togo Cultural Week

The TGME association participated in the organization of a cultural week dedicated to Togo from 18 to 22 April 2018 at the former headquarters of the association Time For Africa, via Cesare Battisti 7/A in Udine (activity of which we attach promotional flyer).

SCHOOL PROJECTS

IOur goal is to expand the opportunities for access to the educational offer of children and young people in the village of Avakodja, through the complete reconstruction of 3 classrooms suitable for education.

A CHILD, AN EDUCATION

From 1 to 30 September 2017 and from 1 to 30 September 2018 we have implemented the project "A CHILD, AN EDUCATION", dedicated to the Koubou-Woro primary school complex, in Tchamba prefecture in Togo, to which we have provided school materials for 217 pupils.
From 2018 to 2019 we have provided school materials to 338 students between the ages of 7 and 12, including 228 girls. All this was possible thanks to the contribution of the Bank of Udine.

Food Support for Foreign Students

In collaboration with the Food Bank of Friuli Venezia Giulia, the TGME association offers food support to foreign students with financial difficulties.
