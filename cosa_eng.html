Cooperation

Collaborate with national and international bodies and organisations, public and private entities, universities, research institutes, associations, groups or clubs that apply for them. On the regional territory (Friuli Venezia Giulia) the TGME association provides reception and information about the university (of Udine, Trieste, Gorizia and Pordenone) for foreign students from Africa; provides temporary accommodation for foreign students arriving in Friuli before obtaining a place at the student's home; accompanies them in the procedures for obtaining a residence permit; It also provides assistance for university enrolment and application for scholarship; provides materials (books,videos) to improve the knowledge of the Italian language; gives moral support to foreign students through a system of mutual aid; informs foreign students about their rights and duties to the university. In collaboration with the Food Bank of Friuli Venezia Giulia, the TGME association offers food support to foreign students with financial difficulties (activity of which we attach a document related to food received and delivered).

The volunteer organization TGME - tax code 94144130302 based in the municipality Udine (Ud), is registered in the General Register of Organized Volunteering referred to in Article 5 of the Regional Law 23/2012 at number 1254.
