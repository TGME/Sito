Time For Africa

Time For Africa is a non-profit association founded in Udine in 2005 (Statute). It is formed by a group of people who have been engaged in cooperative and voluntary activities in African lands for years, who share the need to face the growing gap of injustices and inequalities towards the South of the World and in particular of Africa, with the aim of developing programmes and projects of international cooperation and solidarity. Time For Africa

CAAF CGIL

The National Consortium CAAF CGIL is the body that represents the 14 regional CAAF members at the national level. The National Consortium has its own statute and regulation, with a Board of Directors and an Assembly of Members. CAAF CGIL

THE UNIVERSITY OF UDINE

The University of Udine is an Italian state university founded in 1978 on popular initiative, thanks to the collection of more than 125,000 signatures, many of which came after the earthquake of May 6, 1976. The university has peripheral offices in Gemona del Friuli, Gorizia and Pordenone. THE UNIVERSITY OF UDINE

CeVI

CeVI is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded in 1984 with the aim of working for human promotion, for fairer international relations and for sustainable, fair and peace-making sustainable global development. It works in the areas of education, awareness and promotion of development programmes.Il CeVI
